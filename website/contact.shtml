Midlands OSM User Group

Mappa Mercia is a project of the Midlands OSM User Group. Most of our communication and coordination is taking place on the osm-gb-westmidlands mailing list and on our wiki pages on the Openstreetmap website:

  • Mailing List
  • Midlands OSM User Group wiki page
  • Mappa Mercia wiki page

Monthly Group Meetings

The Midlands OSM User Group meets on the first Thursday of the month in the evening (around 7pm) in the Queens Arms in Birmingham Centre. Details about upcoming socials are published on the wiki page of the Midlands OSM User Group and on the mailing list.

Newcomers are welcome! For more information please contact community@mappa-mercia.org.
