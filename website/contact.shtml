OSM Users in the Midlands

Mappa Mercia is a project of the OSM users in the Midlands. Most of our communication and coordination is taking place on a mailing list and on our wiki pages on the OpenStreetMap website:

  • Mailing list
  • Mappa Mercia wiki page

Monthly Group Meetings

The OSM Midlands users meet on the first Thursday of the month in the evening (around 7pm) in a pub in Birmingham. Details about upcoming socials are published on the OSM wiki page of Mappa Mercia and on the mailing list.

Newcomers are welcome! For more information please contact community@mappa-mercia.org.
