Dear Visitor,

you propably read this page because you wondered why the Mappa Mercia website is so ugly and does not work properly.

This has nothing to do with the website but with the web browser that you are using. The Internet Explorer 6 has been released eight years ago and two new versions have been published since then. On the Internet eight years are a small eternity. Standards for designing webpages have improved a lot and it is now possible to create webpages that work across all modern browsers.

However, since the standards have evolved so much your Internet Explorer 6 does not understand them anymore. It is a bit like when you try to read a text written in Middle English. Just the other way round.

I make this website in my spare time for fun. So, I mostly do things on it that I like to do. Spending a day to translate the site from the equivalent of Modern English to Middle English after the website is basically finished and working with all modern browsers is something I do not consider an enjoyable pastime. Therefore I do not do it.

Over 90% of all Internet users today are using web browsers which understand the modern web standards. So, the majority of the users can enjoy the Mappa Mercia website as it is intended. To everyone else I suggest to update their web browsers to enhance their experience of the Internet and to make the live of all web designers easier!

Here are some links to websites where you can download an up-to-date web browser:

  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Opera
  • Google Chrome
  • MS Internet Explorer 8

Thank you!
